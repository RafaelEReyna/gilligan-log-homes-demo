§ 04 — The Gilligan Guarantee

If we can't find it, we'll build it.

For thirty years the Gilligan guarantee has been simple: if the home you're picturing doesn't exist yet in Big Bear, we'll build it for you. We're not just realtors — we're log home builders, and have been since the day we opened.

From custom log cabins to full lakefront estates, Brad and the team have drawn, permitted, framed, and handed over keys to more than a hundred homes across this valley. Bring us a sketch on a napkin. We'll take it from there.
